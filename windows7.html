System Requirements

Minimum:

  • Processor: 1 GHz or faster
  • RAM: 1 GB for 32-bit, 2 GB for 64-bit
  • Disk Space: 16 GB (32-bit) or 20 GB (64-bit)
  • Graphics: DirectX 9 or later with WDDM 1.0 driver
  • Display Resolution: 800 x 600

Recommended:

  • Processor: 2.5 GHz Dual Core or faster
  • RAM: 4 GB for 32-bit, 8 GB for 64-bit
  • Disk Space: 160 GB (32-bit) or 320 GB (64-bit)
  • Graphics: Nvidia or AMD
  • Display Resolution: 1920 x 1080 (FHD)
  • Microsoft Account and Internet access
