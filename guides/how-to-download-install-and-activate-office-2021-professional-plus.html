✅ Before You Begin

  • A Windows PC
  • Internet connection
  • Your license key

💡 Installation Option 1: setup.office.com

  1. Go to setup.office.com
  2. Sign in or create a Microsoft account
  3. Enter your 25-character product key → Click Next
  4. Choose language/version → Click Download
  5. Run the downloaded installer

💡 Installation Option 2: Direct IMG Download

  1. 👉 Download Office 2021 Pro Plus – English (USA)
  2. Mount the .img file (Right-click → Mount or use tools like WinCDEmu)
  3. Run setup.exe from the mounted drive

🔐 Activate Office 2021 Professional Plus

  1. Open any Office app (e.g., Word)
  2. Go to File → Account → Activate Product
  3. Choose “I have a product key”
  4. Enter your 25-character key → Click Activate

✅ If successful, you'll see “Product Activated.”

❓ Troubleshooting Tips

  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify the key and re-enter carefully
  • Try running setup as administrator
