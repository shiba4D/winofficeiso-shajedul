Windows 11 System Requirements

Before diving into the installation process, ensuring your system meets the minimum requirements for Windows 11 is crucial.

RAM and Disk Space Restrictions

Windows 11 requires a minimum of 4 GB of RAM and 64 GB of storage space. For optimal performance, it‚Äôs recommended to have at least 8 GB of RAM and 128 GB of storage.

TPM and UEFI Requirements

Windows 11 requires a compatible TPM (Trusted Platform Module) version 2.0 and UEFI firmware with Secure Boot support. These security features help protect your system from unauthorized access and ensure a more secure computing environment.

Special System Requirements

  • Intel Core or AMD Ryzen processor (8th generation or later)
  • TPM 2.0 chip installed and enabled in BIOS
  • UEFI Secure Boot enabled in BIOS
  • At least 64 GB of storage space
  • Minimum 4 GB RAM (8 GB recommended)

The requirement of at least an 8th-generation CPU, TPM, and Secure Boot has made Windows 11 inaccessible for many older devices. To enjoy Windows 11 fully, you may need a modern laptop or PC.
