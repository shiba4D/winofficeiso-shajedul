How to Download & Install
  1. Go to office.com and sign in.
  2. Click “Install Office” and select your version.
  3. Follow prompts to download & install.
  4. For offline installer, choose “Other install options” → “Download an offline installer”.
Uninstalling Office 2019
  1. Open Control Panel → Programs and Features.
  2. Select Microsoft Office 2019 → “Uninstall”.
  3. Follow prompts to complete removal.
